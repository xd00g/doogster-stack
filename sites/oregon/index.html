Day by day

The final route

FRI · AUG 14
Eugene

Land, settle in, stock up

Collect the car, check into Hayward Inn and buy water, breakfasts and trail snacks. Dinner at Sabai or 5th Street Public Market.

SAT · AUG 15
Eugene → Waldport

Pacific Sourdough + Ford House

Drive to Waldport and collect a prepaid Pacific Sourdough order before its Saturday closing time. Settle into Ford House; Grand Central Pizza makes an easy arrival dinner.

SUN · AUG 16
Newport + coast

Rockfish charter + Traci’s morning

One-person Yaquina Bay charter from 7 a.m.–noon. Traci chooses a private Ford House morning, Bread & Roses plus the 804 Trail, or a Newport aquarium morning. Reunite for a gentle Cape Perpetua afternoon.

MON · AUG 17
Waldport → Glide

Scenic transfer

Follow US-101 to Reedsport, stop at Highwater Café, then turn inland. Buy groceries before leaving Roseburg and finish with the retreat hot tub.

TUE · AUG 18
Lower North Umpqua

Glide-area sampler

Colliding Rivers, Deadline Falls and Susan Creek Falls make a flexible lower-mileage forest day.

WED · AUG 19
Crater Lake

Primary Crater Lake day

Leave around 6:30–7 a.m. and allow roughly 2½ hours each way. Focus on Rim Village and open West Rim viewpoints.

THU · AUG 20
Hot springs / backup

Recovery day—or weather pivot

Use today for Crater Lake if Wednesday is smoky or cloudy. Otherwise choose Umpqua Hot Springs if access is open, one waterfall, or a full retreat day.

FRI · AUG 21
Glide → EUG

Airport morning

Leave around 8 a.m. for the 1 p.m.-ish flight, preserving time for fuel, rental return and security.

Optional day trip

Crater Lake: Wednesday first

Budget about five hours of driving plus your time at the rim. Wednesday, Aug. 19 is the primary day; Thursday, Aug. 20 is the smoke and visibility backup.

GO / NO-GO RULE
Check visibility and roads Tuesday evening. If the lake is obscured, switch to Thursday.

No timed entry is currently required. Summer vehicle admission is $30. Cleetwood Cove is closed in 2026, so there are no boat tours, swimming or legal shore access.
