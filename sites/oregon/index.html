The published 7 a.m. time is departure. The operator requests arrival 30–45 minutes early.

Directions to check-in
Parking

Charter row by the store

Park in the CHARTER row directly north of the store entrance. Avoid Long Term/Moorage spaces. Free passes are limited to the first 25 vehicles; otherwise buy the $9 Port pass or use legal street parking. Boats are on G dock.

Weather

Watch your phone Saturday

If conditions prevent departure, the office expects to contact guests by roughly 3–4 p.m. the day before. Departure and return times can shift.

License

Buy at check-in

One-day angling license including shellfish: $29. Required 2026 Ocean Endorsement: $4 daily. Total: $33.

Catch services · cash

Cleaning at the marina

Rockfish $2 each, lingcod $4 each or more for extra-large fish, and clean/cook crab $2 each. Independent workers set the final prices.
